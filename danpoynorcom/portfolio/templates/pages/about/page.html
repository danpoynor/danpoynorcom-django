Biography (200 words)

Dan Poynor is an American multidisciplinary designer based in Austin, Texas with twenty plus years experience working primarily in print and interactive mediums. His body of work ranges from projects for global technology companies such as AOL, Cisco, HP, and Yahoo to projects for small startups such as BusinesSuites, Insightly, LeadsCon, and Storm Ventures.

He graduated from California Institute of the Arts with a Bachelor’s Degree in Visual Communications in 1990. Post graduation he began his career with two internships in Holland and then as a junior graphic designer working in Hollywood, California. As new media and the internet emerged in the ’90s Dan moved to the San Francisco Bay Area and embraced new technology while driving efforts on cutting edge communication platforms. Over the course of his career he has worked in both analog and digital environments and has participated on projects as large as the brand identity for the European Union.

Dan defines design as the ability to control a medium and apply thoughtful and responsive messaging which communicates effectively. With this intent he has developed above average skills over the years for all necessary production, design, and mangement needs in print and interactive arenas.
